about me

Wonder Woman is a fictional superheroine,
appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics.
The character first appeared in All Star Comics #8 in October 1941
and she is a founding member of the Justice League.
In her homeland, the island nation of Themyscira, her official title is Princess Diana of Themyscira.
in another version, she is Diana, the Princess of the Amazons (based on Greek mythology),
that has superhuman abilities and tools given to her by the Olympic gods,
in order to bring peace, love and brotherhood to a divided world.
In the latest adaptation of the character, she received her characteristics and powers from the gods -
beauty like Aphrodite, wisdom like Athena, physical power like Demeter,
and animal instincts like Artemis.
When blending into the society outside of her homeland,
she sometimes adopts her civilian identity Diana Prince.
